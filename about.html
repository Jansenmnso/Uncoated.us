About Uncoated

The MNSO Uncoated Podcast hosts members from across the United States and internationally to cooperate, build bridges, and challenge political extremism.

Discussions range from the political climate in the United States to broader global perspectives. The goal is not to push one ideology, but to examine issues from multiple viewpoints and promote informed conversation.

The MNSO works with Republicans, Democrats, and Independents alike — seeking to understand different perspectives and encourage productive dialogue.

Through discussion and collaboration, the goal is simple: work toward building a stronger and more unified America.
